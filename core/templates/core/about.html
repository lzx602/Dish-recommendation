About This Platform

Intelligent Dish Discovery & Recommendation Platform

This platform uses Collaborative Filtering to suggest dishes based on your behaviour and the preferences of users with similar tastes. The more you rate, the smarter your recommendations become.

Who is it for?
  • Busy Professionals / Students — who struggle to choose what to eat
  • Food Enthusiasts — who want to explore new tastes
  • Restaurant Administrators — who manage digital menus
Problems We Solve
  • Too many options — no more endless scrolling
  • Generic recommendations — your ratings change what you see
How the Recommendation Engine Works
  1. You rate dishes (1–5 stars) as you use the platform.
  2. The system identifies other users with similar rating patterns (User-Based Collaborative Filtering).
  3. Dishes that similar users rated highly — but you haven't tried — are recommended to you.
  4. The more you rate, the more accurate your personalised list becomes.
